my travel bucket list

  1. Bali

    Bali is famous for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, ancient temples.
    Highlights include Pandawa Beach behind limestone cliffs, now famous for its white sand.
    Bali offers something for everyone.


    Official Bali Tourism

  2. Rishikesh

    Rishikesh is situated on the banks of the sacred Ganges River.
    The Laxman Jhula bridge is a landmark connecting the two sides of the city and offering panoramic views of the river and surrounding hills.
    The city is renowned for its yoga centers, meditation retreats, and vibrant evening Ganga Aarti


    Uttarakhand Tourism

  3. Chandrashila

    Chandrashila is a peak offering breathtaking 360-degree views of some of the highest Himalayan mountains, including Nanda Devi and Trisul.
    The trek to Chandrashila passes through Tungnath, the highest Shiva temple


    Uttarakhand Tourism
